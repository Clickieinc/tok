About Tournament of Kings

The Tournament of Kings is a grand tournament, powered by gamers from all over the world, founded on a philosophy of gamer empowerment.

We seek a unitary goal and future for gamers everywhere, giving them the tools, training, and means to success. While there can only be one king or queen in the end, every participant will win the skills necessary to transform what is more or less a casual hobby into an actual career path.

The tournament seeks to crown one person as the ultimate gamer, but like in history, crowns are won through talent and hard work not by any divine right. Anyone and everyone who can play video games is welcome to join, from the most casual of gamers to the hardest of the hardcore. Monarchs are crowned on their merits, and even the lowliest amongst us can cast the largest shadows.
