About Tournament of Kings

A new era has dawned; the sun of casual gaming has set. Many will enter the Tournament of Kings. Countless more will fall. But in the end, there can be only one. One winner, one grand prize; one King or Queen, to lord over all the other lesser gamers.

In cities and states all across these United States, gamers from every background imaginable will gather and fight for honor, for glory, for the chance to become legend--but most importantly, for the ultimate prize. Do you have what it takes to win, to rule, to achieve your destiny?
